Financial Education App

For my final project at my internship with Envestnet I created a financial education app using react native. As a company we saw the problem that underserved communities and millennials have inadequate access to financial education and aimed to create a solution to this problem with an app.

We interviewed people around the company and gathered design feedback from their UX team, eventually designing a prototype in figma. I then created a real version of this app using React Native and Redux and presented it to the company as my final project.
