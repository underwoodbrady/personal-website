Red Trade Prototype

Red Trade is a stock portfolio visualization plugin that I created for the Big Idea Challenge to highlight innovation that is possible in customizable dashboards. I designed drag and drop modules to make analyzing a portfolio as easy as possible. This allows for the tracking of specific news feeds, industries, or individual stock performance.

My program gathers information from your preferred brokerage and then displays it in an understandable way, combined with general stock and news data. It is currently a proof of concept but received funding for further development.
