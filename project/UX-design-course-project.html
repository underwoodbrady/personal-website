UX Design Course Project

For my course project in CSCI443 we created a design prototype for a simple bracketing app. The problem we aimed to solve is a lack of support for bracket creation and management for "less popular" sports and competitions.

We worked toward solving this by interviewing a set of people who used bracket management apps in the past and listening to their experiences, we then used this feedback to build out a feature list and create sketches and more detailed prototypes in Figma.
